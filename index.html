Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Answers to common questions regarding your Umrah, Hajj, and Tours.

How long does the visa process take?

Umrah visa processing usually completes within **7 to 10 working days**, but Hajj visa time depends on government procedures. We provide full guidance.

Are the hotels within walking distance of the Haram?

Our **Gold** and **Platinum** packages only include **closest 5-star hotels** to Masjid Al Haram and Masjid An Nabawi, to ensure ease in your worship.

Are meals included in your packages?

Most premium packages include **Buffet Breakfast** or **Half-Board (Breakfast and Dinner)**. Options are available according to your needs.

Is a guide facility available in Ziyarat tours?

Yes, every one of our group packages includes an **experienced and certified Mu'allim/Guide** who provides you with historical and spiritual information.

What sites are included in Madinah Ziyarat?

Standard Madinah Ziyarat includes sacred sites like **Masjid Quba**, **Jabal Uhud**, **Masjid Qiblatayn**, and the gravesite in **Jannatul Baqi**.

Do you offer international tours as well?

Yes, we create custom tour packages for other international destinations, including **Europe (Schengen)**, **Turkey**, and **Southeast Asia (Malaysia/Thailand)**, offering full visa and itinerary support.
