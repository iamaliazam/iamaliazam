Aam taur par zaroori documents yeh hain:

  • **Valid Passport** (Minimum 6 mahine ki validity)
  • **CNIC** ki copy
  • **Passport size photos** (White background)
  • **Health Certificate** (Meningitis vaccination)
  • Women ke liye **Mahram Proof** (Agar 45 saal se kam hain)

Final requirements Pakistan ki Ministry of Religious Affairs ke mutabiq tabdeel ho sakti hain.

Hamare **Gold** aur **Platinum** packages mein sirf woh 5-star hotels shamil hote hain jo Masjid Al Haram (Makkah) aur Masjid An Nabawi (Madinah) se **walking distance** (100 se 300 meters) par hain. Yeh aapki ibadat aur sukoon ke liye bahut zaroori hai.

Jee haan. Har group package mein **experienced aur certified Mu'allim** shamil hote hain jo aapko Umrah/Hajj ke har rukun ko sahi tareeqe se anjaam dene mein madad karte hain. Mu'allim Urdu, English aur Arabic mein rehnumai karte hain.

**Silver** packages mein comfortable **air-conditioned bus** ya luxury coach ka intezam hota hai. **Gold** aur **Platinum** packages mein, hum aapko **private luxury car** (SUV) ya high-speed train tickets provide karte hain taake safar aasani se ho.

Saudi visa policy ke mutabiq, aam taur par 45 saal se kam umar ki khawateen ko **Mahram** (father, brother, husband) ke saath travel karna zaroori hota hai. Lekin 45 saal se zyada umar ki khawateen baghair Mahram ke group ke saath Umrah kar sakti hain. Hum aapko latest policy ke mutabiq guide karenge.

**Umrah** ek chota safar hai jo saal bhar kabhi bhi kiya ja sakta hai aur iske arkaan (rituals) kam hain (Tawaf, Sa'i, Halq/Qasr). **Hajj** Islam ka 5th rukun hai, jo sirf **Zul Hijjah** ke mahine mein kiya jata hai aur ismein Umrah se zyada arkaan (Arafat, Mina, Muzdalifah) shamil hote hain. Hajj packages lambe aur zyada complex hote hain.

Agar aapka sawal yahan nahi mila, toh humse direct baat karein.
